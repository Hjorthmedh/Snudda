To tune a set of neurons do the following:

When in the snudda directory:

tuning/input_scaling.py setup networks/fs_input_scaling/ --cellspecs data/neurons/ --inputType thalamic --numInputMin 100 --numInputMax 1500 --numInputSteps 30 --neuronType fs --inputFrequency [1,2] --inputDuration 10

tuning/input_scaling.py simulate networks/input_scaling_v1/ 

tuning/input_scaling.py analyse networks/input_scaling_v1/


To also plot traces:

python3 plotting/Network_plot_traces.py networks/input_scaling_v1//output_volt.txt networks/input_scaling_v1/network-pruned-synapses.hdf5
